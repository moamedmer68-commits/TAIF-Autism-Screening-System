# ETHICS & CLINICAL SAFETY CARD

TAIF is **not** a medical diagnostic system. Autism Spectrum Disorder diagnosis requires qualified clinical assessment, developmental history, behavioral observation, and specialist evaluation.

## Safety Principles

- Use child codes instead of real names.
- Do not store private child images or audio in public repositories.
- Do not expose API keys.
- Always present outputs as screening indicators, not diagnosis.
- Encourage specialist follow-up when moderate or high risk indicators appear.
